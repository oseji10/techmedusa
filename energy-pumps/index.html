At your service

With more than 100 years of experience in design and manufacturing of specialised pumps for the energy industry, we offer you flexible designs and alternative forms of construction to match your needs. Our long, proven track record in delivering reliable, efficient products is your guarantee that our pump packages are solutions you can trust.

Find our products and solutions within the areas of firewater pump packages, seawater lift pumps, cargo offloading pumps and auxillary pumps as well as global sales and aftersales services below. Do not hesitate to contact us for more information or requests.

Energy Pump Solutions

Fire Water Pump Packages

Our fire water pump packages are designed for offshore market which is under guidelines of NFPA-20 and marine class requirements with different configurations of design.

Read more

Seawater Lift Pumps

Our internally mounted sea water lift pumps which can be installed in the machinery room or in the pump room on conversion vessels to provide the vessel with cooling seawater from the sea chest.

Read more

Cargo Offloading Pumps

Our hard wearing and reliable cargo offloading pumps are designed to be fitted in the pump room. The pumps are fitted with double mechanical seals and tapered shaft coupling for safe operation and easy maintenance.
